Making the Fried rice

  1. Slice and season chicken braest
  2. Fry using olive oil and set aside
  3. Fry the rice and add your chopped bell pepper
  4. Add 1 tea spoon all purpose seasoning
  5. Add the desired amount of water
  6. Stire and allow to cook
  7. Once almost ready add the fried chicken breast and mixeed vegetables
